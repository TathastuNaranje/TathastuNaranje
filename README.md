## ✦ The Renaissance Consultant

I am a Market Intelligence Consultant at Meta, where I decode global categories for procurement leaders. But the discipline of analysis does not end at the spreadsheet. By night, I craft parallel worlds as a speculative fiction author, and translate discipline into sound as a musician.

This GitHub profile serves as my digital garden — a repository for strategic frameworks, world-building lore, and the intersection of analytical work and creative practice.
